```
19. pune, maharashtra. somewhere between an idea and its consequence.

grew up on chess and cricket —
learned early that the game is never just the game.

at 8, sold snacks and whiteners out of a school bag.
at 13, built NMX Esports — a PUBG/BGMI organisation — from nothing.
at 19, running an AI product studio and asking questions
nobody in the room wants to answer.

somewhere between 2021 and 2024, something broke.
and then something opened.

time stopped feeling infinite.
meaning stopped feeling optional.
krishnamurti and osho stopped being names
and started being mirrors.

now i build at the intersection of
AI, consciousness, markets, and whatever comes next.
not because i have the answers.
because i can't stop asking the questions.
```
